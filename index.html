Methodology

This calculator uses the following methodology and data sources to determine daily water intake requirements:

  • Weight: Calculated as one ounce of water for every half pound of body mass, or 33ml per kilogram (UMSystem).
  • Exercise: Adds 12 ounces (or 250ml) of water for every 30 minutes of exercise (UMSystem).
  • Temperature: Adjusts total water needs by 10% for every 5°C deviation from 15°C. Based on temperature multipliers derived from a 2020 study in Environmental Sciences Proceedings (MDPI).
  • Coffee and Alcohol: Adds 240ml (8oz) for every coffee consumed and 500ml (17oz) for every alcoholic drink. (NHS).

Please note that individual water needs may vary based on health, climate, and other factors. This tool provides general guidance only.
